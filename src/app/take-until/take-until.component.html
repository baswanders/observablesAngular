Take until - for easy unsubscribe

The code of this example is based of the "memory leak" example.
We added an other subscribtion, to show the benefits of unsubscribing via TakeUntil.
Like in the "memory leak" example, open your console log. Observe that the counter is running.
Move back to the home page and you see the log stops printing to the console.
Our subscribtion is correctly unsubscribed!
