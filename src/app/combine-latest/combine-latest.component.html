CombineLatest

Here we combine 2 observable streams.
We have two observables, one for getting todos, the other for getting the users.
We wait until we got data from both observables and then do something with the data.

Whenever possible use the async version. Try not to subscribe in the component, use the returned observable directly in the html template with | async

Async version
